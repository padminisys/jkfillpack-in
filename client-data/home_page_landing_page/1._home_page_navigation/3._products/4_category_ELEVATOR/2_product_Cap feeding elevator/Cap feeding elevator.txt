Cap feeding elevator

•	Hopper provided at base in which caps need to be dumped.
•	It works in conjunction with capping machine.
•	Waterfall type cap elevator can store relatively large amount of caps.
•	Purpose of waterfall type cap feeder elevator is to orient screw cap or ROPP caps & discharge it to transfer system which is generally a chute of capping machine.
•	No need of cap sorter of similar cap design/ shape.
•	Cap do not stuck in water fall type elevator.
•	Easy to set and maintain.
