BARREL RINSING MACHINES

•	Used to rinse empty barrel before filling application.
•	Usually used at transformer oil filling machine.
•	After manual centring of bung, barrel will be inverted upside down, rinsing nozzle spray oil inside the empty drum and oil washed out from drum through bung.
•	After few seconds barrel will be inverted in vertical position where bung will be lifted to top side for filling cycle.
•	Barrel will move automatically from infeed conveyor to rinsing station and from rinsing station to out-feed conveyor.
•	Automatic bung centring option will be additional feature which can be incorporate as per requirement.
