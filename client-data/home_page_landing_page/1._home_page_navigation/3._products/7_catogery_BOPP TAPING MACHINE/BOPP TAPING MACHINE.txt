BOPP TAPING MACHINE

•	Used to apply self-adhesive tape on cartons
•	Option of top taping, bottom taping and top & bottom taping at a time is available.
•	Can accommodate different size cartos by adjusting height (vertically) and lateral guide (horizontally).
•	Semiautomatic and fully automatic options are available.
•	Easy maintenance of taping head.
•	Height adjustment legs of ± 50 mm.
•	MOC: MS powder coated or SS304, as per requirement.
•	Power: 230V AC, 50Hz.
